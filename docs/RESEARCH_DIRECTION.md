# Research Direction

## Current direction

The current direction of this repository is narrow by design.

The goal is not to build a total philosophical system.

The goal is to test whether suspending the privileged human observer yields non-trivial structural gains in specific foundational domains.

At this stage, the two primary domains are:

- time
- causality

These were selected because they are both deeply shaped by ordinary human cognition and highly consequential for description, modeling, and theory formation.

---

## Why time

Human thought tends to treat time through lived succession:

- before
- after
- duration
- flow
- memory as axis

This may be useful phenomenologically, but it may not be the best starting point for structural description.

The working hypothesis is that some temporal descriptions may become more general when they are reformulated in terms of:

- ordered differences between configurations
- dependency between states
- partial ordering
- local rather than universal temporal structure
- transformation-density rather than lived flow

### Time research question

**What remains of temporal structure when lived human succession is no longer treated as the default primitive?**

---

## Why causality

Human thought strongly favors narrative causality:

- one thing causes another
- linear sequence
- readable direction
- identifiable source

This is often cognitively efficient, but many real systems may be better described through:

- distributed constraints
- conditional dependencies
- recursive interaction
- circular or multi-source determination
- configuration-level stability

### Causality research question

**What remains of causal structure when the human need for a readable directional story is no longer treated as the default primitive?**

---

## Why these two domains come first

Time and causality are not only important topics.

They are also especially useful test domains because:

1. They are foundational.  
   Many other concepts depend on them.

2. They are observer-sensitive.  
   Human formulations often build them from local phenomenology.

3. They are structurally rich.  
   Suspending anthropic defaults may reveal non-trivial alternatives.

4. They are comparable.  
   Standard human formulations can be contrasted directly with de-centered reformulations.

---

## Current method

The repository currently uses Protocol O1:

**Suspension of the privileged observer**

For each target formulation, the method is:

1. identify the standard human formulation
2. identify the implicit observer assumption
3. identify the distortion introduced
4. produce a de-centered reformulation
5. describe the structure that emerges

This method is exploratory, not final.

Its function is to determine whether there is enough structural gain to justify stronger formalization.

---

## Near-term objectives

The immediate objectives are the following:

### Objective 1
Build a small but strong set of reference cases for time.

Examples may include:
- time flows
- the past is fixed
- the future does not exist yet
- events happen in sequence
- duration is what is felt between two changes

### Objective 2
Build a small but strong set of reference cases for causality.

Examples may include:
- A causes B
- the event happened because of X
- the system failed because component Y broke
- one decision changed everything
- one variable explains the outcome

### Objective 3
Compare standard observer-centered descriptions against de-centered reformulations and identify recurring structural motifs.

Possible recurring motifs:
- frame dependence
- state-ordering
- distributed constraint
- conditional stability
- non-primitive objecthood
- narrative compression

---

## What would count as progress

This repository should only grow if one of the following happens:

1. observer suspension consistently reveals structures hidden by observer-centered language
2. repeated de-centered reformulations converge toward reusable descriptive patterns
3. those patterns support stronger formalization
4. the method generates genuinely new conceptual or analytical leverage

If none of this happens, the project should remain a limited exploratory archive.

---

## What this repository is not doing yet

At this stage, the project is **not** yet attempting:

- a universal formal language
- a complete ontology
- a mathematical closure
- a final theory of time
- a final theory of causality

Those would be premature.

The present goal is narrower:

**test whether the suspension of the privileged observer produces enough structural gain to justify deeper formal work.**
